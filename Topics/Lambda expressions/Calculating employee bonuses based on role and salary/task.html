Calculating employee bonuses based on role and salary

Hey there! This problem might be a bit unpredictable, but give it a go and let us know how you do!
A company needs an application to calculate employees' bonuses. The bonus is calculated as a percentage of the employee's salary and varies based on their role (Managers get 15%, Team Leads get 10%, and Others get 5%). Your task is develop a program that takes as input an employee's role and salary, and returns the bonus amount. Assume that the role (Manager, Team lead, or Other) is entered as a string and the salary as an integer.
Sample Input:
Manager
1000

Sample Output:
150


Sample Input:
Team Lead
2000

Sample Output:
200



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
